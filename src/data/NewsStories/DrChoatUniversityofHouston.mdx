**Date: 09/30/2025**

Cho Lab’s PI, **Dr. Eunsang Cho**, visited the University of Houston to give an invited talk “Advancing Snow Hydrology with Remote Sensing, Modeling, and Data Assimilation,” as part of the Geo-sensing Graduate Seminar Series hosted by Dr. Hyongki Lee. It was fantastic time to meet and discuss potential collaborative opportunities with UH faculty members and grad students.
