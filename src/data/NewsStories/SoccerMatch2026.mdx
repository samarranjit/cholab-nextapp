# Cho Lab Members Enjoy Friendly Soccer Match

  src="/images/news/SoccerMatch2026/GroupPhoto.jpeg"

Date: 03/12/2026

The Cho Lab wrapped up the week before spring break with a friendly soccer match among team members on Friday, March 12. The match was played between two teams led by two of our graduate students, Himmat and Farhaan. It was a fun event filled with teamwork and friendly competition, helping strengthen the team spirit among lab members.

Everyone had a blast this time! And after an exciting game, Team Farhaan squeaked out a narrow victory!

The Cho Lab looks forward to organizing more activities like this in the future that not only lighten the mood but also allow members to connect beyond research.
