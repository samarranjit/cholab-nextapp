# Dr. Cho Joins Editorial Board of Nature’s Scientific Reports

**Date: 10/01/2025**

PI Dr. Eunsang Cho has joined the Editorial Board of **Scientific Reports (Nature Portfolio)** as of October 2025. Scientific Reports is a peer-reviewed, open-access, multidisciplinary journal published by **Nature Portfolio (Springer Nature)**, covering a broad range of fields across the natural and environmental sciences, engineering, and more. It is one of the world’s most widely cited open-access journals and provides a highly visible platform for disseminating robust, technically sound research.

Dr. Cho’s appointment to the Editorial Board reflects his growing contributions to hydrology, remote sensing, climate extremes, and environmental data science, and it also strengthens Cho Lab’s engagement with the broader international research community.
