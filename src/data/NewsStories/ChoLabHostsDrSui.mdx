# Cho Lab Hosts Dr Sui’s Seminar on Urbanization’s Impacts on Hydroclimate Extremes

**Date: 10/22/2025**

On October 22, the Cho Lab hosted **Dr. Xinxin Sui**, a postdoctoral researcher at the **Colorado School of Mines** (Dr. Andy Wood’s group), for her informative seminar titled “Urbanization’s Impacts on Hydroclimate Extremes: From Knowledge to Actionable Science.” It was a special occasion for her, as this was the first invited talk she has delivered, and for us as well, as she was our lab’s first invited speaker.

Our lab members had the opportunity to interact with Dr. Sui over lunch and discuss topics related to her current and future research projects. The

Cho Lab is so thankful to Dr. Sui for enlightening us with her work and inspiring us to further explore the hydroclimatic impacts of urbanization.
