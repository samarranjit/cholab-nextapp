**Date: 11/16/2025**

We are thrilled to announce that one of our undergraduate researchers, Hrishant Sharma, has been awarded the **Undergraduate Research Fellowship (URF)** from the **College of Science and Engineering, Texas State University** — a highly competitive award given to only about 10–12 students each semester.
His award-winning project is titled:

> “Benchmarking Deep Learning Models for Sub-Seasonal Streamflow Forecasting: A Case Study in the Yellowstone River Basin.”

He mentioned _"I am truly grateful to Dr. Eunsang Cho and all lab members who have supported me throughout this journey. Your guidance and encouragement have played a major role in my growth as a researcher."_

**Congratulations, Hrishant!**
