Date: 10/03/2025

The ChoLab keeps up with its tradition for a semesterly Lunch Meeting where Dr. Cho and the students meet over lunch bringing them together for community, mentorship, and lively research chats.

The Graduate Research Students met with Dr. Cho on 11th November and the Undergraduate Students had a simliar meeting over lunch on 3rd October. Both of the meetups were filled with chats, gossips, discussions about experiences and future plans related to lab and lab activities.
