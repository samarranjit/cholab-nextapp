Date: 10/03/2025

Every semester, Dr. Cho and our amazing students gather for a cozy **Lunch Meeting** - a time to connect, share mentorship, swap stories, and dream big about research (plus enjoy some great food!).

Because of limited shared availability this fall, the Cho Lab met twice on separate dates. Both lunches were full of laughter, friendly chats, shared experiences, and exciting conversations about life and research in the lab.
